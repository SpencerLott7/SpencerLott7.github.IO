Todd's Adventure

This project was the very first Unity project I created in my first year with GIMM. It is a basic side scrolling 2-D platforming game that has you traversing platforms and collecting berries on your quest to free the princess from the snakes. The overall Unity project took me about two months and the game trialer displaying it took about two weeks. This project being my first experience with Unity definatly surprised me with what I could make. I learned a lot about project timelines and the implementation of my ideas into game mechanics and story aspects.
